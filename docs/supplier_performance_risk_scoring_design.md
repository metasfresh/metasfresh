# Supplier Performance and Risk Scoring Framework

## Objective
Enhance procurement decision-making by introducing a supplier performance and risk scoring system that enables data-driven supplier selection and improves supply chain resilience.

## Problem
Current metasfresh procurement workflows lack supplier intelligence features such as:
- No visibility into supplier reliability or performance history
- No risk-based supplier selection
- Limited support for strategic sourcing decisions

This leads to:
- Suboptimal supplier selection
- Increased risk of delays or disruptions
- Inefficient procurement planning

## Proposed Solution

### 1. Supplier Performance Metrics
Introduce tracking for:
- On-time delivery rate
- Order fulfillment accuracy
- Lead time variability
- Historical delay patterns

### 2. Risk Scoring Model
Develop a scoring mechanism based on:
- Supplier reliability trends
- Geographic or operational risk
- Historical performance data
- Supply consistency

### 3. Integration with Procurement Logic
- Use supplier scores in purchase order decision-making
- Recommend optimal suppliers based on score + cost + lead time
- Flag high-risk suppliers during procurement planning

### 4. Decision Intelligence Layer
- Provide real-time insights during procurement execution
- Enable automated supplier ranking and recommendations

## Expected Impact
- Improved supplier selection accuracy
- Reduced supply chain risk
- Better alignment between procurement and real-world supply conditions
- Enhanced decision-making using data-driven insights

## Field Relevance
This contribution demonstrates expertise in:
- Supply chain risk management
- Supplier performance analytics
- Procurement optimization strategies

It extends metasfresh capabilities by introducing intelligent supplier evaluation mechanisms aligned with modern supply chain practices.
